I'm Tom, a developer and designer for the web, with my eventual aim to be an iOS developer and designer. I have recently completed my A Levels at a Sixth Form in North Hampshire, UK and am attending Brunel University to study Computer Science in September 2013. During the Summer, I am working at Rawnet as a Junior Web Developer.

A goal which I want to achieve at some point in my life is to work for Apple (or Facebook, Google, Microsoft), but I suspect this may change in the next few years as I also hope to become increasingly involved in the startup culture.

I co-founded a design agency, 5 Lines in September 2011. We specialise in designing and developing bespoke sites for small businesses and enjoy taking our clients through the whole journey of making a website.
