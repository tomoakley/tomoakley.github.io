I'm Tom, a developer and designer for the web, with my eventual aim to be an iOS developer and designer. I am a Year 13 Student (making me 18 years old) at a Sixth Form College in Hampshire (South UK) (last year of High School to you Americans).

A goal which I want to achieve at some point in my life is to work for Apple (or Facebook, Google, Microsoft), but I suspect this may change in the next few years as I also hope to become increasingly involved in the startup culture.

I co-founded a design agency, 5 Lines in September 2011. Right now I'm a front-end developer and designer there during the time when I'm not doing college work.
